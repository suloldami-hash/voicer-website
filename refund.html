1. General Refund Rules

We believe in the strict operational capability of the Voicer Boom Edition. However, if you are experiencing critical system failures or the macOS application completely fails to run on a supported architecture environment (macOS Sequoia), we offer a 14-day refund window from the exact date of your purchase vector.

2. Conditions for Refund

To qualify for a refund, users must physically demonstrate that the application has a fundamental hardware or software logic conflict that our support engineers cannot resolve manually. Change of mind does not qualify as a valid refund criteria due to the immediate digital deliverance of the compiled software.

3. Request Pipeline

To initiate the refund operation, please contact our support desk directly with your Lemon Squeezy order ID and a brief breakdown of the structural failure. We process all refunds purely through the Lemon Squeezy merchant of record protocol.
