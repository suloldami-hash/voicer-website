How can we help?

Find answers to common questions or reach out to our engineering team directly.

How do I get an API key?

Voicer uses your own API credits to keep the app cost low and your data private. You can generate a key at OpenAI or Groq.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Voicer records audio locally, sends it directly to your chosen API provider, and clears the buffer immediately. We never see your recordings or your transcripts.

Which macOS versions are supported?

Voicer is optimized for macOS 14 (Sonoma) and macOS 15 (Sequoia). It runs natively on both Intel and Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) Macs.

How do I update the app?

Voicer includes Sparkle-based automatic updates. You'll receive a notification when a new version is available, or you can check manually in the settings panel.
